AC TWO WHEELER

8th of JUNE 2020 10.15AM

Have you ever thought? That an AC can be fixed in two wheeler!!! Yes Daniel Kim had done this.

The most important feature of the Lit Motors C-1 is its gyroscopic technology means it stay in perfect balance even in the case of an accident. The vehicle is helped to remain upright by a set of electronically-controlled gyroscopes located under the floor.Thats why its under the category of AEV(Auto balancing Electric Vehicle).

It is not clear what Apple's plans for Lit Motors was. However, Lit Motors has been keeping a very low profile recently. The company's last post on their Twitter page was made back in October of 2016. Also, Lit Motors is not taking any more pre orders for the C-1 on their website from Jun 15, 2017. APPLE UPDATE

9th of June 10:34 AM

Hear is the new Official Update form Apple

Across the world, governments and health authorities are working together to find solutions to the COVID-19 pandemic, to protect people and get society back up and running. Software developers are contributing by crafting technical tools to help combat the virus and save lives. In this spirit of collaboration, Google and Apple are announcing a joint effort to enable the use of Bluetooth technology to help governments and health agencies reduce the spread of the virus, with user privacy and security central to the design.

So this is very first time that Apple and Google both are doing this project together.Since COVID-19 can be transmitted through close proximity to affected individuals, public health officials have identified contact tracing as a valuable tool to help contain its spread.A number of leading public health authorities, universities, and NGOs around the world have been doing important work to develop opt-in contact tracing technology.This techenology will be one of the best tech in Apple and Google.

This whole thing will be created on the Xcode(IDE for Apple product like, Macbook and Ipads) and Programming languages will be Swift(this language is for making apple applications) and Objective-C(One of the best language amoung other).
